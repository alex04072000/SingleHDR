Results on the HDR-Synth, HDR-Real, RAISE, HDR-Eye dataset, and examples from prior work and DxOMark.

We provide the full comparison with all evaluated methods in the paper.

We use the Vibrant tone-mapping mode in Photomatix (https://www.hdrsoft.com/) to generate the tone-mapped HDR images.

The "Ground truth HDR" images in the HDR-Real dataset are generated from fusing multiple LDR images with Photomatix. Therefore, there might have a slight misalignment between the ground truth HDR and input LDR image.
